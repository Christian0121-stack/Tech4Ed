Bookkeeping NC III

National Certificate III

The Bookkeeping NC III program equips trainees with competencies in maintaining a complete set of books of accounts manually and through a computerized accounting system. Graduates are prepared for employment in accounting departments, business process outsourcing firms, and government agencies.

🕐 Duration
292 Hours
📋 Qualification Level
NC III
🏛️ Issued By
Philippine Government
💼 Sector
Business & Management

📌 What you will learn

  • Maintain a complete set of books of accounts manually
  • Prepare financial statements and reports
  • Use computerized accounting systems
  • Process payroll and government-mandated contributions
  • Record and summarize financial transactions

Trainee Requirements Bookkeeping NC III — Please prepare the following documents
📋 Basic Requirements
  • Duly accomplished Registration Form
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate (PSA / NSO)
  • 2x2 ID photo (recent, white background)
  • Photocopy of a valid government-issued ID
🎓 Educational Requirements
  • High School Diploma or equivalent (Form 137 / Form 138)
  • Certificate of Completion for ALS graduates
📝 Additional Documents
  • Barangay Clearance or Certificate of Residency
  • Medical Certificate (fit to work / fit to study)
  • Endorsement letter from Barangay Captain (if applicable)
💻 Technical Requirements
  • Basic computer literacy is recommended
  • Trainees must be at least 18 years old
  • Must be a resident of Balanga City (priority)
